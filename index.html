About Me

I'm a recent graduate from Carnegie Mellon University, where I got my masters in Artificial Intelligence Engineering - Mechanical Engineering. The goal was to learn how AI will play a role at the intersection of science and engineering.

Prior to graduate school, I worked as a flight test engineer at The Boeing Company, where I worked in various roles across the flight test engineering discipline. I've also worked as a design engineer intern at Parker Hannifin, where I developed flight control actuation designs.

Prior to my industry experience, I completed two undergraduate degrees in Mechanical Engineering and Aerospace Engineering from the University of California, Irvine. I also started pursuing a private pilot license during this time.
